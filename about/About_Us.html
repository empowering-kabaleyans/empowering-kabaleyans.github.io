College Education

Dedicated to empowering deserving students from Santa Barbara, Pangasinan, by providing financial assistance to help them achieve their educational dreams.

Community Engagement

SBPAAI brings together stakeholders and volunteers through projects like Pamalabas Ya Gawat and Uyos na Kaban, which provide relief and support to poor but deserving individuals in Santa Barbara. These initiatives highlight our commitment to active social involvement and community empowerment.

About Us

A California nonprofit public benefit corporation to engage in activities to foster fellowship among its members, to promote their common cultural interests, and to render assistance to the inhabitants of Santa Barbara, Pangasinan, Philippines.

The Santa Barbara Pangasinan Alumni Association, Inc. (SBPAAI) is a testament to the enduring spirit of community, solidarity, and giving back. Established 40 years ago, SBPAAI was born out of a shared commitment to remember our roots and uplift the lives of our fellow Santa Barbara town folks. Over the decades, our association has become a beacon of hope and a platform for camaraderie, channeling the collective efforts of members, stakeholders, and friends to create meaningful change.

Our journey has been marked by challenges and triumphs, guided by the values of teamwork, hard work, and dedication. Through dynamic leadership and the unwavering support of our members, SBPAAI has grown into an organization that not only fosters social connections but also mobilizes resources to assist the marginalized members of our community.

As we always keep with our quote, "Tree of Dreams, Together We Build," it truly embodies the spirit of collaboration that defines SBPAAI. It highlights our relentless pursuit of mobilizing kindness and generosity to empower the youth and others to realize their dreams for a brighter future.

As we honor the past, we pay tribute to the tireless efforts of our past presidents and leaders who have shaped our organization, ensuring its viability and success. Their dedication and vision have inspired collective action and a legacy of community building that continues to thrive. SBPAAI is more than an association; it is a family united by shared roots and the dream of a better tomorrow. Together, we remain committed to making a difference, one act of kindness at a time.
