Step 1: Pre-Application

Applicants fill out a Google Form that contains their names and contact numbers. The contact details will be used to contact them for scheduled interviews and actual Google Forms for application.

Step 2: Application

Applicants will be contacted through the contact details that they have provided during the pre-application period. Applicants will receive the Google Form link through the number they provided on the pre-application form and will be directed to answer them only in a specific timeframe.

Step 3: Interview

Applicants are subjected to the final phase of the application through the interview. Applicants are advised to attend the interview during the schedule they have received during the pre- application phase.

Step 4: Initial Shortlisting / Screening

The team of interviewers will make a shortlist from all of the applicants. Their form responses will be summarized and will be sent to the US end for further evaluation. Shortlisted applicants are advised to have their lines open for further questions and clarifications.

Step 5: Final Selection

The US end will receive the shortlist of applicants from the Team of interviewers from Santa Barbara. Board of Directors will choose this year's SBPAAI CSP scholars through further deliberations, inquiries, and clarifications.
