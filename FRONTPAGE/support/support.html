HOW TO SUPPORT US USING GCASH QR

1. Open Your GCash App: Launch the GCash application on your smartphone. If you haven’t already, make sure to log in to your account.

2. Select ‘Pay QR’: From the dashboard, tap on the ‘Pay QR’ option. This may also appear as ‘Scan QR Code’ depending on your app version.

3. Scan the QR Code: Use your phone’s camera to scan the QR code provided for donations. Ensure the QR code is clearly visible for easy scanning.

4. Enter the Amount: Once the QR code is recognized, you will be prompted to enter the amount you wish to donate.

5. Confirm Your Donation: Review the details of your transaction to ensure everything is correct. Then, confirm your donation by tapping the ‘Pay’ button.

6. Successful Donation: You will receive a confirmation message on your screen once the donation is successful. Keep this confirmation for your records.
