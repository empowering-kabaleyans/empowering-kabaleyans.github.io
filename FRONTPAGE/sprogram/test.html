Step 1: Pre-Application

Applicants fill out a Google Form that contains their names and contact numbers. The contact details will be used to contact them for scheduled interviews and actual Google Forms for application.

2

Step 2: Application

Applicants will be contacted through the contact details that they have provided during the pre-application period. Applicants will receive the Google Form link through the number they provided on the pre-application form and will be directed to answer them only in a specific timeframe.

3

Step 3: Interview

After submitting their applications, applicants will undergo an interview process, which will be scheduled based on their availability.

4

Step 4: Selection

Following the interview, the applicants will be evaluated based on the criteria set for the program, and selected candidates will be notified.

5

Step 5: Awarding

The final step involves the awarding ceremony for selected recipients, where they will receive recognition and support for their educational pursuits.
