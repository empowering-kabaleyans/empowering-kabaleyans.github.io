Santa Barbara Pangasinan Association of America, Inc., commonly known as SBPAAI, is a Filipino non-profit organization that aims to empower our Kabaleyans from Santa Barbara to become communities of active citizens by mobilizing the largest volunteer network in implementing Bayanihan programs.
